What is Shearwater Productions?

Shearwater Productions is a soon-to-be production studio for movies and TV shows, but is currently the personal brand for me, Ben Vallee. I am studying television and media production and hope to one day create my own production studio to create original works alongside creative and talented people. What Shearwater Productions currently offers are some small short films, skits, demo reels, and scripts as well as a sneak peak into what I hope to make Shearwater Production’s first full project, a TV Series called “Vainglorious”, which you can learn more about on the Vainglorious page.

The latest information surrounding Shearwater Productions, you can head right to the Blog page, which will feature the most recent and important news.
